Career Goals

Towards the start of my career, I would like to be something like a product amanger at a large tech company. Eventually, I want to climb higher in the ranks, and lead the company or a subsidary.

If I don't want to work at a company like that, I will probably launch my own startup and lead it as the CEO. I know that I want to combine my passion for business and computer science in a way that makes me proud of myself, while keeping myself happy.

College Plans

I want a major in computer science and a minor in business. I want to go to Stanford because they have a great business school, and a great computer science program.

My alternative is getting a masters in computer science, and working for a while before coming back to get an MBA. I could also try to get into the MET program at UC Berkeley to get a double major in business and comp sci. I would also love to go to Carnegie Mellon University for Computer Science. If I take a break between business and comp sci, I would probably try to go to a university near me, so if I'm in california, I'll try to go to Stanford. If I'm in the east Coast, schools like NYU, Harvard, or maybe Wharton would be a better idea for the MBA.
